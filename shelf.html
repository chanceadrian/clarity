Your ideas and memories come to life in stunning color and timeless forms. Navigate your collection by swiping through spaces or tapping through tunnels. Give your mood boards and late-night musings a better place to live. Designed for introspection.

Whether on the way to class, a first date, or simply lounging at home, cabinet is designed for spontaneity. Add as fast as you think — or change themes on a whim. And it comes with its own archive.

All kinds of artifacts.
And expanding.

Artifacts let you write up poetic ballads, record future songs and fiery rants, or hold onto that future job you’ve been eyeing. They sit three to a shelf, and besides a flower or two, capture life in every way it happens.

Tunnel, Flower, Photo, Radio, Delivery Basket, Time Capsule, Links (3 styles), Note Stack, & Note.

Take note
on life.

Write poems to the ones you love, or just get something off your chest. Notes supports all the features you expect — rich text, bullet points, and photos, too. Got a lot to say? Just stack two notes together to create a note stack.

Say it like it is.

Sometimes our voices just say it better. Rather than obscure emotions in lengthy, written paragraphs — let them sing. And with voice transcripts, it's easy to skip to the best parts of that thirty minute rant.

Save a dream job
or two.

Links live tucked away inside little computers, with three styles to choose from: iMac, Mac, and Mac Color. Save your favorite corners of the web, the kind that could determine your future — or maybe that couch for your dream home or apartment.

Frame something wonderful.

Display your best family photos, or build a moodboard for a new painting. Live photos and video automatically play, and when you add more than one, Shelf automatically picks the best to display throughout the day. And like any item, add a caption to save and edit passing thoughts.

Designed to expand.

Need more room? Add a tunnel or two. Tunnels feature the shelf icon you're about to enter, and you can even write subheadings to focus in on any topic. Like a folder. Unlike any folder.

Gift anything. Even a shelf.

The Shelf Postal Service is designed for the ones we trust. Send an artifact to a trusted friend; items get delivered in baskets, and deliveries are left to you on hooks.
