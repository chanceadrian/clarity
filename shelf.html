Design

Inspired by the beauty and simplicity of shelves in our homes, Shelf feels familiar yet utterly unique. Its bold and brilliant take on color invites you to rethink, reshape, and reorganize. This is journaling elevated to an art form—one actually a part of the creative conversation.

Magic Cabinet

Cabinet is built into every shelf, making it easy to change style or add new artifacts to your collection. If your shelves overflow, older items get safely tucked away in the cabinet archive.

Labyrinths of Thought

Tunnels inspire introspection by drawing one inward both visually and metaphorically, created to make your innermost thoughts as easy to find as a Van Gogh in MoMA. Tunnel titles help you group main ideas. They're like their own rooms.

Your Metropolitan Museum of Art

There is no limit to the amount of tunnels you can have in Shelf. You can even make a whole room of tunnels if you like. And in the odd chance that a space capable of holding 1,944 artifacts isn't enough, just create another space!

Exploring Interaction

Could Shelf decorate our homes? It just might. What if you could write a hand written letter on a desk and walk it over to its right place? Or rip it up to delete it? What if when you put your ear to a voice memo, it starts to play? Welcome to spatial computing.

Knock it Down

What if you could knock over a shelf and watch your stuff fall onto the ground? Maybe you could pick up an idea, a literal idea, and turn it over in your hands. Place it somewhere new, like tidying your room. In Shelf, the future of emotional design is wide open.
